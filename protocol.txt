===Small protocol definition for clipshare===

1. client broadcasts clipshare HELO packet to everybody on port 1234 with contents:
CSHELO:ip:port:OLEHSC
2. other clients see this packet and add a socket to their internal list of sockets 
based on the IP and port
3. if something changes to the clipboard of one of the clients, he/she sends this change
to all the clients in the client list
